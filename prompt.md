# UI/UX Refinement Prompt – Campaign Type Template Only

You are a Senior UI/UX Designer, Political Campaign Brand Designer, and Frontend Engineer.

Your task is to refine **only the Campaign Type Template** (the green template with the large background typography, vertical divider, candidate name at the bottom-left, supporter/candidate photo cards on the right, and quote panel).

## Primary Objective

Upgrade this template from **v1.0 to v1.5** through professional UI/UX refinement.

This is **NOT** a redesign.

The existing composition, layout, branding, and visual identity are already approved.

Your responsibility is to polish the design while preserving its recognizable appearance.

---

# DO NOT

* Change the overall layout.
* Move major sections.
* Change the template identity.
* Replace the green campaign branding.
* Reduce the vibrant campaign colors.
* Introduce gray, beige, muted, or editorial color palettes.
* Break html-to-image export compatibility.
* Modify responsive scaling.

---

# Preserve

Keep:

* Bright green campaign identity.
* White campaign border.
* Large background typography.
* Vertical center divider.
* Candidate name placement.
* Two-photo composition.
* Quote panel location.
* Footer branding.
* Overall campaign energy.

---

# 1. Background Refinement

Do not redesign the background.

Instead:

* Reduce the opacity of the oversized background letters slightly so they support the layout without competing with the foreground.
* Improve the gradient transitions.
* Add subtle depth using soft radial lighting.
* Keep the background bright and energetic.

Avoid making the background darker or dull.

---

# 2. Vertical Divider

The divider is visually too dominant.

Refine it by:

* slightly reducing its width
* softening the pure white appearance
* introducing a subtle gradient or gentle shadow
* making it feel integrated rather than floating

Keep it as an important design element.

---

# 3. Candidate Name

Improve typography hierarchy.

Refine:

* optical alignment
* line spacing
* letter spacing
* visual balance

Keep the bold campaign typography.

Add only a subtle shadow if necessary.

Do not use glowing effects.

---

# 4. Photo Cards

Maintain the current placement.

Improve:

* border consistency
* corner radius (slightly if appropriate)
* photo framing
* soft layered shadows
* depth

The cards should feel premium while remaining campaign-focused.

---

# 5. Photo Labels

Improve the small labels:

"THE VISION"

"THE VOTER"

Increase:

* padding
* spacing
* alignment
* readability

Ensure the labels feel intentional and premium.

---

# 6. Quote Panel

The quote panel currently feels too flat.

Refine it by:

* increasing internal padding
* improving typography
* introducing a subtle gradient
* adding a refined left accent bar
* improving depth with a soft shadow

Do not relocate the panel.

Keep the campaign aesthetic.

---

# 7. Footer Badge

Refine the "Powered by HON. HASH 2027" badge.

Improve:

* spacing
* integration with the border
* shadow
* padding

It should appear as part of the overall design instead of an attached element.

---

# 8. White Border

Maintain the border.

Improve its finish by:

* ensuring consistent thickness
* improving corner precision
* making it appear crisp at all export sizes

---

# 9. Visual Hierarchy

Ensure the viewer naturally looks in this order:

1. Candidate Name
2. Candidate Photo
3. Supporter Photo
4. Quote
5. Campaign Label
6. Footer Badge

No element should overpower the candidate's identity.

---

# 10. Depth & Premium Finish

Introduce premium polish using:

* layered shadows
* refined gradients
* subtle highlights
* optical spacing
* consistent border thickness
* better alignment

Avoid excessive glow effects.

Avoid glassmorphism.

Avoid overly decorative effects.

---

# 11. Color Direction

Maintain the vibrant campaign palette.

Primary colors:

* Campaign Green
* White
* Black
* Small Gold accents (optional)

Do NOT convert the template into a gray, muted, monochrome, or newspaper-style design.

The final result should feel:

* modern
* energetic
* optimistic
* premium
* campaign-focused

---

# Technical Constraints

* Preserve html-to-image compatibility.
* Do not introduce unsupported CSS filters.
* Do not affect export quality.
* Do not modify responsive behavior.
* Do not change canvas dimensions.

---

# Final Goal

The refined template should look like the same design—but professionally art-directed.

Users should immediately recognize it as the original template, only cleaner, more premium, more balanced, and visually stronger.

The result should feel like an official 2027 campaign asset ready for social media, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, X, and high-resolution print.
